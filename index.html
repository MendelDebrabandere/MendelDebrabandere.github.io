Projects in progress / upcoming projects

Graduation work

I am currently working on my final project in DAE, my Graduation work.

I chose to work on "Open world optimizations in Unreal Engine 5".
I will investigate the systems in place to allow giant scale terrains (10x10 km). What are the trade-offs that need to be made for optimization, how do you guarantee that areas in the far distance look like correct representations of their close up counterparts.
These are the systems I will measure and compare: LOD, Nanite, World Partitioning, Streaming Virtual Texturing, Mip-based Texture Streaming and finally look at lighting and shadows.
I will make measurements on perfermance to see what systems have the biggest impact on large worlds, and what systems fail on a big scale.
